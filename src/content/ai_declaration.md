# AI Usage Declaration

## Purposes
LLMs were used to research and inform architectural decisions for this project. The models were also userd to advise on specific coding queries, to teach the developer some basic SVG concepts and generate early iterations of the AI Transparency Badge itself. The usage licence for the AI Transparency Badge was also generated by AI with human and AI review and refinement.

## Model
GPT-5.5 Instant and below, accessed via ChatGPT free plan on web.

## Data Sources
The model had internet access during execution.

## Scope(s)
Direct and indirect artifacts of AI are spread throughout this project: HTML/CSS/JS/SVG code and versions of the license agreement.

These artifacts were generated at the time of coding. AI is not 'in-the-loop' during runtime (while the website is serving requests) - the website is completely static to the developer's knowledge.
